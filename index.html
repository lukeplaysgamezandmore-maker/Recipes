🍋 Lemonade Recipe

You Need:

  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps:

1. Juice the lemons and remove seeds.
2. Add about 1 tablespoon of sugar per lemon. Stir well.
3. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes so sugar dissolves.
4. Add 3 cups of cold water. Stir.
5. Taste and adjust (add more water or sugar if needed).

Tip:

For every 1 cup of lemon juice, use 3–4 cups of water.

Enjoy your lemonade! 🍹
